Ibile Youth Academy

The IBILE YOUTH ACADEMY offers various programmes that build the Health and Physical, Personal and Social, Cognitive and Creative, Vocational and Citizenship/Civic Competencies of the participants. The Academy emphasizes learning strategies based upon fun, play, action group and individual challenges teaching life skills rather than academic lessons.

The initiative had been held consistently over the past four years and had produced State Youth Ambassadors and Divisional Ambassadors who had implemented various pet projects/ programmes to address peculiar issues in their community.

The Lagos State Youth Leadership Initiative was established in Year 2017. It is designed as a training platform to inculcate in the youth the virtues of Citizenship, Leadership and Volunteerism; designed to ignite young people's personal development and transformation required for bright future and community development.
